Comprehensive Real Estate Management Made Easy

Efficiently Manage, Rent, and Maintain Your Properties

  • Rent Collection: Simplify rent payments with secure online transactions.
  • Tenant and Service Provider Management: Find tenants and connect with service providers seamlessly.
  • Document Handling: Send and receive important documents effortlessly.

Manage Rental Unit is an all-in-one real estate management software designed to help property owners efficiently manage their rental units. From collecting rent and finding tenants to connecting with service providers, our software streamlines all aspects of property management. Tenants can create accounts to find and rent units, while service providers can register to offer their services for property maintenance and repairs. With Manage Rental Unit, you can handle all your property management needs in one place, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free experience for both landlords and tenants.
